Puppy Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four puppies below. By clicking on a puppy, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total amount of puppies to a number of puppies that is randomly generated. You lose the game if your total number of puppies goes above the random number of puppies.

The value of each puppy is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time, when the game starts, the game will change the values of each puppy.
